About Shree Nachiketa Vidya Sankul Gujarati Medium School.

Shree Nachiketa Vidhya Sankul was founded in July 2015 at Rajula, Amreli by the NandKishor Cheritable Trust. The School was started with Nursery and primery on its inception and today we have our own building and have High school and Higher Secondary Section up to 12th Std. (Science Streams) Comprising of more than 100 children on roll. All the Sections of Nachiketa Vidhya Sankul have been accorded recognition by the Govt, of Gujarat.

The main aim and objective of our institution is to prepare better citizens for tomorrow and years to come. Needless to say the School is Primarily meant for your children and we would appeal to you, dear parents, to come forward and help us in easing our burden. A little gesture on your part will go a long way, putting the institution on solid foundations.Nand Kishor Trust is also actively involve din various Social works. Blood donation camp was organized by the School in which more than 300 people have extended their co-operation by donating blood. Nand Kishor Trust has added yet another feather in its cap by starting, a sports club with its head office at Rajula. This club conducts different sports events such as Volley ball, Cricket, Table Tennis, Bad Minton etc.

We unfold the stimulated notions and explore and abundance of Excellency through our pedagogical aspects to reach the heights of fathoms of ocean.
