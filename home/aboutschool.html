Our InfraStructure

The ergonomically designed infrastructure enhances the teaching and learning environment. The classrooms are IT enabled and equipped with audio visual aids like smart boards, projectors etc that make learning interactive and enjoyable. There is hundred per cent power backup and an RO filtration plant for drinking water.The school has a multipurpose hall equipped with hi-tech audio visual equipment, for hosting school events which can double up as a hall for hosting tournaments and indoor games.

We love what we do and it's our passion!

Nachiketa Vidhya Sankul feels socially responsible to grow children physically, socially, spiritually & intellectually. Hence, we are focused at developing the roots of fresh untouched brains through our batches, Pre. Primary, Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary.
